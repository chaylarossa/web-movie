Lovely Runner

In the midst of her despair, top star Sun-jae is Im Sol's only hope. However, one day, she hears the shocking news that Sun-jae has tragically passed away. Her world is turned upside down, and she wakes up in tears, only to find herself in the past—15 years ago, when Sun-jae was still alive. Sol seizes this opportunity and is determined to do everything she can to save Sun-jae. Back then, Sun-jae was just a student dedicated solely to swimming. But how could Sol, who didn’t even know of his existence until yesterday, now know his name?
